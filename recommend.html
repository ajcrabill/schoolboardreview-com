Recommend a Board for Review

Know a school board whose governance deserves a closer look — good or bad? We rely on public records and verbatim transcripts. Tell us who to review next.

What makes a good candidate?

  • Board meetings are recorded and publicly available
  • Transcripts or auto-captions exist (or meeting videos do)
  • The board has adopted written student outcome goals
  • Publicly available meeting schedule or archives
  • Any board — high-performing or struggling — qualifies
  • District size doesn't matter; governance quality does
